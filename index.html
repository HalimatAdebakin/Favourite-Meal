STIR FRY SPAGHETTI

ABOUT THIS MEAL

Spaghetti is a cylindrical pasta that is long, thin, and solid. It is a popular dish in traditional Italian cuisine. Spaghetti, like other pastas, is made of milled wheat and water, and is sometimes fortified with vitamins and minerals. Durum wheat semolina is commonly used to make Italian spaghetti. Because refined flour is used, the pasta is usually white, but whole wheat flour may be added. Spaghettoni is a thicker type of spaghetti, whereas capellini is very thin. Stir frying is a Chinese cooking technique that involves frying ingredients in a small amount of very hot oil while stirring or tossing them in a wok. The technique originated in China and has since spread throughout Asia and the Western world. In Western cooking, it is similar to sauteing. Stir frying and Chinese food have been recommended as both healthy and appealing due to their skillful use of low-fat vegetables, meats, and fish, as well as sauces that are not overly rich, provided calories are kept to a reasonable level. Pasta makes a quick dinner or lunch box dish for your kids. It is healthy and filling at the same time, plus your kids will love it for sure. And you can almost add anything, any vegetables in the pasta. Pasta is quite bland on its own, so it picks up the flavours which you add beautifully.

INGREDIENTS

  • 100 grams Spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 6 cloves Garlic chopped very finely
  • 1 medium Onion sliced thinly
  • 1 medium Carrots cut thinly lengthwise
  • ½ Capsicum thinly lengthwise
  • 2 tsp Red Chilli Flakes
  • 1 tsp Oregano
  • ¼ cup Olives I used black & green
  • 2 tsp Soy Sauce
  • 2 tbsp Parsley finely chopped
  • Salt to taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat lots of water in a sauce pan, once it reaches a boil, add in salt. Add in pasta and cook till it is done. Once it is cooked drain and set aside. While the pasta is cooking start making the spicy veg mix.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan, add in garlic and saute for a min.
  3. Add in onions and saute for a min.
  4. Add in carrots and capsicum and saute for 10 mins or so till it browned and cooked.
  5. Add in chilli flakes, oregano, salt and soy sauce. Mix well.
  6. Add in olives and toss once.
  7. Add in cooked drained pasta and mix well.
  8. Add in parsley and mix.
  9. Serve.
